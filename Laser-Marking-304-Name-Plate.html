Laser Marking 316 and SS 304

SS 316 and SS 304 Laser Marking Name Plates are premium stainless steel nameplates where text, logos, and designs are marked using laser technology. These nameplates are widely used in industrial, commercial, and branding applications due to their durability, precision, and resistance to corrosion.

Laser Marking Process

  • 01Fiber Laser Marking:
    • Uses high-powered laser beams to create permanent marks.
    • No additional chemicals or inks required.
    • Produces high-contrast, durable markings.
  • 02CO₂ Laser Marking (With Coating):
    • Requires a special coating on stainless steel before marking.
    • Not as durable as fiber laser marking.

Advantages of Laser Marking Name Plate

  • Permanent Marking – The text/logo does not fade over time.
  • Corrosion & Heat Resistant – Ideal for extreme conditions.
  • Precision & Clarity – Sharp, detailed engraving.
  • Low Maintenance – Easy to clean and maintain.
  • Customizable – Various sizes, fonts, and designs available.
